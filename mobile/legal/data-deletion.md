# Data Deletion Instructions - UnionEyes

If you wish to delete your account and personal data from UnionEyes, you have several options:

## Option 1: In-App Deletion (Recommended)

1. Open the UnionEyes app
2. Navigate to **Settings** ‚Üí **Account**
3. Scroll to bottom and tap **Delete Account**
4. Confirm your identity (password or biometric)
5. Review what will be deleted
6. Tap **Permanently Delete Account**
7. Confirm deletion

Your account and personal data will be deleted within 30 days.

## Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to: **privacy@unioneyes.com**

**Subject:** Account Deletion Request

**Include:**

- Full name
- Email address associated with account
- Union membership ID (if available)
- Reason for deletion (optional)

We will respond within 3 business days and complete deletion within 30 days.

## Option 3: Phone Request

Call: **1-800-UNION-EYE**

Say: "I want to delete my account"

Provide:

- Full name
- Email address
- Verify your identity

## Option 4: Written Request

Mail a written request to:

**UnionEyes Privacy Team**  
[Company Address]  
[City, State ZIP]

Include:

- Full name
- Email address
- Signature
- Date

## Data Export

Before deleting, you can export your data:

1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Privacy** ‚Üí **Export Data**
2. Select data types to export
3. Tap **Request Export**
4. Receive download link via email (within 24 hours)
5. Download your data (link valid for 7 days)

Exported data includes:

- Profile information
- Claims history
- Uploaded documents
- Communication logs
- Settings and preferences

## Reactivation

After deletion:

- You cannot reactivate your account
- You must create a new account
- Previous data cannot be recovered
- New account starts fresh
