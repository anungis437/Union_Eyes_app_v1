# Age Rating Questionnaire Guide - UnionEyes

Guide for completing age rating questionnaires for App Store and Play Store.

## Overview

Age ratings ensure your app is accessible to appropriate audiences and helps parents make informed decisions. Both app stores require completing questionnaires about app content.

## Apple App Store Age Rating

### How to Complete

1. Go to App Store Connect
2. Select your app
3. Go to App Information → Age Ratings
4. Answer questionnaire honestly

### UnionEyes Recommended Answers

#### Violence

- **Cartoon or Fantasy Violence:** No
- **Realistic Violence:** No
- **Prolonged Graphic or Sadistic Realistic Violence:** No
- **Sexual Content or Nudity:** No
- **Profanity or Crude Humor:** No

#### Sexual Content & Nudity

- **Infrequent/Mild Sexual Content or Nudity:** No
- **Frequent/Intense Sexual Content or Nudity:** No

#### Horror & Fear

- **Infrequent/Mild Horror/Fear Themes:** No
- **Frequent/Intense Horror/Fear Themes:** No

#### Medical/Treatment Information

- **Medical/Treatment Information:** No
- **Drug/Alcohol/Tobacco Reference or Use:** No

#### Mature/Suggestive Themes

- **Infrequent/Mild Mature/Suggestive Themes:** No
- **Frequent/Intense Mature/Suggestive Themes:** No

#### Simulated Gambling

- **Infrequent/Mild Simulated Gambling:** No
- **Frequent/Intense Simulated Gambling:** No

#### Contests, Sweepstakes & Other

- **Contests:** No
- **Unrestricted Web Access:** No (app doesn't include a web browser)
- **Gambling and Contests:** No

### Expected Age Rating: **4+**

This is the lowest age rating, indicating the app is suitable for all ages.

## Important Notes

### Accuracy is Critical

- Answer all questions honestly
- Misrepresentation can lead to app removal
- Consider edge cases (user-generated content)
- When in doubt, disclose rather than hide

### Union-Specific Considerations

**Employee Information:**

- Contains employment data: Not age-restricted
- No sensitive personal data visible to other users

**Claim Documents:**

- Medical records: Private, not viewable by others
- No graphic medical content displayed

**Communication:**

- Messaging with union staff only
- No public forums or social features
- All content moderated

### Updating Age Ratings

**When to Update:**

- Adding social features
- Allowing user-generated public content
- Adding in-app purchases
- Adding advertising
- Adding web browser
- Any content changes

**How to Update:**

- Apple: App Store Connect → Age Ratings → Edit
- Google: Play Console → Content Rating → Start New Questionnaire

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Don't:**

- Skip questions
- Provide inaccurate information
- Assume content is okay because it's professional
- Forget about user-generated content
- Ignore communication features

✅ **Do:**

- Answer every question carefully
- Consider all app features
- Think about user interactions
- Review privacy implications
- Update when features change

---

## Appeals Process

### If Rating is Higher Than Expected

**Apple:**

1. Contact App Review Board
2. Explain app functionality
3. Provide evidence of appropriate content
4. May take 1-2 weeks

**Google:**

1. Contact Play Console Support
2. Request re-evaluation
3. Provide detailed explanation
4. May take 2-3 business days

---

## Regional Variations

### Special Considerations

**European Union (PEGI):**

- Stricter on privacy
- GDPR requirements
- May require additional documentation

**Germany (USK):**

- More conservative ratings
- Focus on violence and scary content

**Australia (ACB):**

- Requires classification
- Higher scrutiny on gambling

**China:**

- Requires government approval
- Additional content restrictions
- Special process for app stores
