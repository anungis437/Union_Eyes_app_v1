# Privacy Policy - UnionEyes Mobile Application

**Effective Date:** February 9, 2026  
**Last Updated:** February 9, 2026

## 1. Introduction

UnionEyes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, do not use the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

**Account Information:**

- Full name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Union membership ID
- Employment details (employer name, position, department)
- Profile photo (optional)

**Claim Information:**

- Claim submissions and details
- Supporting documents and receipts
- Medical, dental, or healthcare records (when applicable)
- Expense reports and invoices
- Reimbursement history
- Payment information (processed through secure third-party processors)

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:**

- Device type and model
- Operating system and version
- Unique device identifiers (UDID, IMEI)
- Mobile network information
- IP address
- App version and settings
- Language preference

**Usage Information:**

- App features used
- Screen navigation patterns
- Button clicks and interactions
- Session duration and frequency
- Search queries
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics

**Location Information (Optional):**

- Approximate location (city, region)
- GPS coordinates (only when explicitly granted)
- Location is only accessed when app is in use
- Used for location-based services and claim validation

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties

- Authentication data from Clerk
- Analytics data from Sentry and Amplitude
- Cloud storage from AWS S3 (encrypted)

We use collected information for the following purposes:

### 3.1 Service Provision

- Process and manage claims

- Send service-related notifications
- Facilitate communication with union staff

### 3.2 Improvement and Analytics

- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns and trends
- Debug errors and fix crashes
- Develop new features
- Conduct research and analysis

### 3.3 Security and Fraud Prevention

- Ensure platform security
- Prevent fraudulent claims
- Detect and prevent unauthorized access
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights and property

### 3.4 Communications

- Send claim status updates
- Provide important announcements
- Respond to inquiries and requests
- Send promotional materials (with consent)

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Security Measures

- **Encryption:** End-to-end encryption for data transmission (SSL/TLS)
- **Encrypted Storage:** All sensitive data encrypted at rest
- **Biometric Authentication:** Face ID, Touch ID, fingerprint protection
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access and authentication
- **Regular Audits:** Security audits and penetration testing
- **Secure Infrastructure:** SOC 2 compliant cloud providers

### 5.2 Data Retention

- **Active Data:** Retained for duration of union membership
- **Archived Claims:** Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for 2 years
- **Deleted Data:** Permanently removed within 30 days of deletion request

### 5.3 Data Location

- Data primarily stored in United States
- AWS data centers with redundancy
- Compliance with applicable data protection laws

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control

- **Access:** Request copy of your personal data
- **Correction:** Update or correct inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your account and data
- **Export:** Download your data in portable format
- **Restriction:** Limit how we use your data
- **Objection:** Object to certain data processing

### 6.2 Communication Preferences

- **Opt-out:** Unsubscribe from promotional emails
- **Push Notifications:** Disable in app settings or device settings
- **Analytics:** Opt-out of analytics tracking in app settings

### 6.3 Location Services

- Control location access in device settings
- Withdraw location permission anytime
- App functions without location access (with limited features)
