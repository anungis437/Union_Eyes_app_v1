# Privacy Policy - UnionEyes Mobile App

**Last Updated:** February 9, 2026

## Introduction

UnionEyes ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

- Name, email address, phone number
- Union membership details
- Employment information
- Profile photo (optional)

### Claim Information

- Claim submissions and documents
- Receipt and expense images
- Medical/dental records (if applicable)
- Reimbursement history

### Device Information

- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version and settings

### Usage Information

- App interaction data
- Feature usage analytics
- Error logs and crash reports
- Performance metrics

### Location Information (Optional)

- Approximate location for services
- Geographic data for claim validation
- Location only when app is in use

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Process and manage claims
- Authenticate your identity
- Send notifications and updates
- Improve app functionality
- Provide customer support
- Ensure security and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

- End-to-end encryption
- Secure data transmission (SSL/TLS)
- Encrypted local storage
- Biometric authentication
- Regular security audits
- Secure cloud infrastructure

### Data Retention

- Active data: Duration of membership
- Archived claims: 7 years (legal requirement)
- Analytics data: 2 years
- Deleted data: Permanently removed within 30 days

## Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

- **Authentication:** Clerk (auth.clerk.dev)
- **Analytics:** Sentry, Amplitude
- **Cloud Storage:** AWS S3
- **Push Notifications:** Expo Notifications
- **Payment Processing:** Stripe (if applicable)

These services have their own privacy policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate information
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of communications
- Withdraw consent

## Children's Privacy

UnionEyes is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. We'll notify you of significant changes via app notification or email.
