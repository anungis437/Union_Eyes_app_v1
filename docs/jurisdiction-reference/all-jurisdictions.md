## 3. Strike Vote Thresholds

Percentage of votes (or members) required to authorize strike action.

| Jurisdiction | Threshold | Calculation Base | Legal Reference | Notes |
|--------------|-----------|------------------|-----------------|-------|
| **Federal** (CA-FED) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Canada Labour Code ¬ß87.3 | Simple majority |
| **Alberta** (CA-AB) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Relations Code s.72 | Simple majority |
| **British Columbia** (CA-BC) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Relations Code s.54 | Simple majority |
| **Manitoba** (CA-MB) | **65%** | Votes cast | Labour Relations Act s.68 | ‚ö†Ô∏è **Super-majority** (highest threshold) |
| **New Brunswick** (CA-NB) | **60%** | Votes cast | Industrial Relations Act s.35 | Higher than simple majority |
| **Newfoundland** (CA-NL) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Relations Act s.89 | Simple majority |
| **Northwest Territories** (CA-NT) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Standards Act s.38 | Simple majority |
| **Nova Scotia** (CA-NS) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Trade Union Act s.36 | Simple majority |
| **Nunavut** (CA-NU) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Standards Act s.38 | Simple majority |
| **Ontario** (CA-ON) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Relations Act ¬ß79 | Simple majority |
| **Prince Edward Island** (CA-PE) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Act s.22 | Simple majority |
| **Quebec** (CA-QC) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Code Art. 20.2 | Simple majority |
| **Saskatchewan** (CA-SK) | **45%** | **All eligible members** | Labour Relations Act s.6(2) | ‚ö†Ô∏è **Unique calculation** (see notes) |
| **Yukon** (CA-YT) | **50%+1** | Votes cast | Labour Standards Act s.38 | Simple majority |

**Key Notes:**
- üî¥ **Manitoba (65%)**: Highest threshold in Canada - super-majority required
- üü† **New Brunswick (60%)**: Higher than standard but lower than Manitoba
- üü° **Saskatchewan (45%)**: UNIQUE - calculated as % of **all eligible members**, not votes cast
  - Example: Union has 1,000 eligible members. 500 vote, 450 vote "Yes"
  - Other jurisdictions: 450/500 = **90%** ‚Üí ‚úÖ PASSES
  - Saskatchewan: 450/1,000 = **45%** ‚Üí ‚úÖ PASSES (but just barely)
- üü¢ **11 jurisdictions**: Standard 50%+1 simple majority
