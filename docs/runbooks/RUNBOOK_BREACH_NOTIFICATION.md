# Runbook: Breach Notification

**Severity:** High
**Estimated Time:** 1-4 hours
**Requires:** Legal and compliance coordination

## Symptoms
- Confirmed data breach
- Regulatory notification requirements triggered

## Diagnosis
1. Confirm scope, affected data, and timeline.
2. Identify impacted tenants and users.

## Resolution Steps
1. Inform legal and compliance teams.
2. Draft notification content using approved templates.
3. Notify stakeholders and regulators per policy.
4. Log all communications and actions.

## Verification
- [ ] Notifications sent to required parties
- [ ] Evidence and timelines documented

## Post-Incident
- [ ] Review notification process for gaps
- [ ] Update policies if needed

## Escalation
If notification deadlines are at risk, escalate to executive leadership.
