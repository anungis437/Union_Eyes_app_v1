### Question 2: "What if the committee finds bias in our AI models?"

**Answer:** **This is exactly why we need the committee.**

**Scenario:** Q2 fairness audit reveals 8% bias in Claim Outcome Prediction (favoring certain demographics).

**Our Response (30-day remediation):**

1. **Immediate:** Suspend affected predictions, notify impacted members
2. **Week 1:** Root cause analysis (data imbalance? feature selection? model architecture?)
3. **Week 2-3:** Model retraining with balanced dataset, bias detection algorithms
4. **Week 4:** Re-audit, validate bias eliminated, resume predictions
5. **Ongoing:** Add continuous bias monitoring, quarterly re-audits

**Transparent Communication:**

- Notify members: "We discovered and fixed a bias issue"
- Explain remediation: "Here's what we did to ensure fairness"
- Demonstrate accountability: "We're committed to ethical AI"

**Result:** Increased trust (we caught it and fixed it) vs. decreased trust (we ignored it and got exposed).

**The alternative (no governance):** Bias goes undetected ‚Üí member lawsuit ‚Üí union reputation damaged ‚Üí regulatory scrutiny ‚Üí forced to shut down AI program.

### Question 4: "How do we know the AI won't replace stewards?"

**Answer:** **AI is designed to augment stewards, not replace them.**

**What AI Does:**

- ‚úÖ Reads 50,000 documents in seconds (legal research)
- ‚úÖ Predicts claim outcomes based on historical data
- ‚úÖ Drafts initial grievance language (steward edits)
- ‚úÖ Flags members at churn risk (steward intervenes)

**What AI Cannot Do:**

- ‚ùå Build trust with members (requires human empathy)
- ‚ùå Navigate workplace politics (requires emotional intelligence)
- ‚ùå Make ethical judgments (requires human values)
- ‚ùå Negotiate with employers (requires human persuasion)
- ‚ùå Provide emotional support (requires human connection)

**The Steward Role Evolution:**

**Before AI:**

- 70% administrative (paperwork, research, data entry)
- 30% member interaction (listening, advocating, supporting)

**After AI:**

- 30% administrative (review AI outputs, approve decisions)
- 70% member interaction (more time for human connection)

**Result:** Stewards spend **more time** doing what they do best (helping members) and **less time** on tasks machines do better (data processing).

**Analogy:** Calculators didn't replace accountants. They made accountants more productive and let them focus on strategy instead of arithmetic. AI is the calculator for stewards.
