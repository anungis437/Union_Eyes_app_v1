1. **Immediate Actions** (Within 1 hour)
   - Isolate affected systems
   - Activate incident response team
   - Call insurance hotline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX

2. **Notification** (Within 24 hours)
   - Notify policy broker
   - File preliminary claim
